About Us

Where Every Dream
Becomes Reality

Nestled in the heart of Nasheman-e-Iqbal Phase 2, Lahore, Sapphire Palace Event Complex stands as a monument of luxury and grandeur. We have been crafting unforgettable memories for families across Pakistan since our inception.

Our meticulously designed halls blend timeless elegance with modern sophistication — from our starlit crystal ceilings and floral arches to our enchanting waterfall features and lush garden setups.

  • Four uniquely designed luxury event halls
  • Dedicated bridal room in every hall
  • Starlit crystal ceiling & cinematic lighting
  • Stunning waterfall & garden feature
  • Ample parking with valet service
  • Exclusive bridal shooting palace

FAQs

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is the maximum guest capacity?+
Our largest hall, Sabbi Hall, accommodates up to 600 guests. Across all four halls we host events from 50 to 600 guests.
Does every hall have a dedicated bridal room?+
Yes — every hall at Sapphire Palace includes a private, luxuriously appointed bridal suite for your preparation and comfort.
Do you offer catering and decoration services?+
Absolutely. We offer premium in-house catering by our master chefs and a full luxury decoration service. Our décor team brings your vision to life.
Is parking available at the venue?+
Yes. We feature a large dedicated parking area with professional valet parking service for all your guests.
Can we book for a pre-wedding photoshoot?+
Yes! We have a dedicated Shooting Palace — beautifully designed for bridal shoots, cinematic videography and pre-wedding sessions.
How far in advance should I book?+
We recommend 3–6 months in advance for weddings. We also accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability.
Where exactly is Sapphire Palace Event Complex?+
We are at Shamim Ul Haq Chowk, Nasheman-e-Iqbal Phase 2, Lahore — easily accessible from all major areas of Lahore.
